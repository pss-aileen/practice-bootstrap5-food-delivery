Register Your Shop

A: To register your restaurant, visit our website and click on the "Register Your Restaurant" link. Fill out the required information, including your restaurant's name, address, and contact details. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you within a few business days.

A: By registering your restaurant, you can reach a larger customer base, increase your sales, and gain access to our marketing and promotional tools. Additionally, you'll have access to our real-time order management system and customer support.

A: There is no upfront registration fee for restaurants. We operate on a commission-based model, where a small percentage of each order is taken as a fee. Details about our commission rates will be provided during the registration process.
